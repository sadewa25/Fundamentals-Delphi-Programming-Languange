3. Menghitung Body Mass Index (BMI)
TB = Tinggi Badan
BB = Berat Badan
BMI = (BB) / (TB) * (TB)

Sample, BB = 70 kg dan TB = 165 cm, maka  BMI = (70) / (1.65) * (1.65) = 25.71

less than 18.5 Underweight
18.5 to 24.9 Normal weight
25 to 29.9 Over weight
30 to 34.9 Obesity 1
35 to 39.9 Obesity 2
more than 40 Obesity 3
